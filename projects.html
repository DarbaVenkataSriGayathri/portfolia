Title of Project

  

STREET LIGHT MONITORING

The Main aim of the project is to save the energy by putting the lights of the system when the system detects movements of an object The system swithes on the street a head of the movement of an object and switches off


Disadvantages of Existing System
  • Existing street light system switched on and off manually
  • Existing street light system has a hig power consumption and their maintenance is also quite expensive
  • More man power is required to handle the functionality of the existing street light system

Proposed System
  • Street lights can switch ON and OFF automatically
  • Maintenance of street lights leads to cost reduction
  • Reduce Light Pollution
  • Power consumption is quite low in these street lights also leads to energy conservation
  • No large man power is required to handle the functionality of these lights
